Pro Tips for Landlords

High-Quality Photos

Take photos during daytime with good lighting. Show each room from multiple angles.

Competitive Pricing

Research similar properties in the same area to set a competitive and realistic price.

Be Accurate

Provide accurate information about the property size, condition, and included amenities.

Properties with quick response times get more inquiries and faster bookings.
