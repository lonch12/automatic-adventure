ABOUT EDEN

Your Trusted Partner in Finding Quality Accommodation

Eden Boarding House was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide comfortable, affordable, and safe accommodation for students and professionals in Zambia. What started as a single boarding house has grown into a trusted platform connecting hundreds of landlords with quality tenants.

We understand that finding the right place to call home is more than just a transaction – it's about finding a space where you can thrive, study, work, and build your future.
