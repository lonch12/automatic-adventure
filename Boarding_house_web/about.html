About Eden Boarding House

Your trusted partner in finding the perfect home since 2015

Our Story

Eden Boarding House was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide comfortable, affordable, and safe accommodation for students and professionals in Zambia. What started as a single boarding house in Lilayi has grown into a trusted platform connecting hundreds of landlords with quality tenants.

We understand that finding the right place to call home is more than just a transaction – it's about finding a space where you can thrive, study, work, and build your future. That's why we're committed to offering properties that aren't just rooms, but true homes.
